METHODS

“Three Tips: Simplify, Simplify, Simplify”. - Jakob Nielsen

The secret to successfully conducting UX research is not to let a lack of time or budget stop you. It's easy to ignore research and jump right into designing, but if you do, you're ignoring your own user.

Design Thinking

It is the architecture on which the thought processes that aim at innovation and the development of new ideas are established, always within a framework of action that pursues customer satisfaction in the most direct way. the delivery of value.

Lean UX

It is a method that allows you to create products that meet all user requirements, reducing the possibility of failure, speeding up your production time and achieving the desired objectives in a shorter time.

User-Centered Design

is a design philosophy that aims to create products that meet the specific needs of its end users, achieving the highest satisfaction and the best possible user experience with minimal effort on your part.

Sometimes they forget tasks they have already done,

For them we have designed habitum, an app that is very easy to use, and that keeps it simple so as not to distract them
